# Discovery Assessment Questions

## Overview

The Discovery Assessment consists of **two parts**:
1. **Destination Discovery** (25 questions) - Understanding where the client wants to go
2. **Service Diagnostic** (15 questions) - Understanding operational needs for service matching

Total: **40 questions**

---
