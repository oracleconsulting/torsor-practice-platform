## 🎯 What It Does

### **Automated Role-Fit Analysis**
Calculates weighted scores for each team member across 4 dimensions:
- **Advisory Suitability** (0-100) - Client-facing capability
- **Technical Suitability** (0-100) - Specialist depth
- **Hybrid Capability** (0-100) - Balanced versatility
- **Leadership Readiness** (0-100) - Management potential

### **Red Flag Detection**
Automatically identifies critical misalignments:
- **Critical:** EQ < 55 for advisory roles, EQ < 60 for leadership
- **High:** Communication/Belbin mismatches
- **Medium/Low:** Development opportunities

### **Development Prioritization**
Generates personalized recommendations:
- **Immediate (0-3 months):** Critical gaps
- **Enhancement (3-6 months):** Performance improvement
- **Excellence (6-12 months):** Leadership development

### **Team Composition Scoring**
- **Belbin Balance Score** - Identifies role gaps/overlaps
- **EQ Collective Capability** - Team emotional intelligence
- **Motivational Alignment** - Driver compatibility
- **Conflict Style Diversity** - Resolution capacity
- **Overall Team Health Score** (0-100)

## 📊 What the Scores Mean

### **Role-Fit Scores (0-100)**
- **80-100:** Excellent fit - high performer in this role
- **60-79:** Good fit - capable with room for growth
- **40-59:** Moderate fit - training recommended
- **0-39:** Poor fit - consider role adjustment

### **Team Health Score (0-100)**
- **80-100:** Excellent team composition
- **60-79:** Good team balance, minor improvements
- **40-59:** Moderate concerns, action required
- **0-39:** Critical issues, urgent intervention

## 🔬 The Science Behind It

### **Advisory Suitability Formula**
```
Score = (EQ Social Awareness × 25%) +
        (EQ Relationship Mgmt × 20%) +
        (People-Oriented Belbin × 20%) +
        (Achievement/Influence × 15%) +
        (Collaborative Conflict Style × 10%) +
        (Sync Communication × 10%)
```

**Ideal:** ≥70 (Good), ≥80 (Excellent)

### **Technical Suitability Formula**
```
Score = (Specialist Belbin × 30%) +
        (EQ Self-Management × 20%) +
        (Achievement Drive × 20%) +
        (Autonomy Drive × 15%) +
        (Attention to Detail Skill × 15%)
```

**Ideal:** ≥70 (Good), ≥80 (Excellent)

### **Hybrid Capability**
```
IF Advisory < 60 OR Technical < 60 THEN
  Score = 0 (Not suitable for hybrid)
ELSE
  Score = (Average × 70%) + (Balance × 30%)
  WHERE Balance = 100 - |Advisory - Technical|
```

**Ideal:** ≥70 (True hybrid capability)

### **Leadership Readiness Formula**
```
Score = (EQ Relationship × 30%) +
        (EQ Social Awareness × 20%) +
        (Leadership Belbin × 25%) +
        (Influence Drive × 15%) +
        (Seniority/Experience × 10%)
```

**Ideal:** ≥70 (Ready), ≥80 (High potential)

### **Team Health Calculation**
```
Score = (Belbin Balance × 30%) +
        (Motivational Alignment × 20%) +
        (EQ Collective × 30%) +
        (Conflict Diversity × 20%)
```

## 🚨 Red Flags Explained

### **Critical (Immediate Action)**
- Advisory role with EQ Social Awareness < 55
- Leadership position with EQ Relationship < 60
- Manager with no leadership Belbin roles

### **High (3-Month Action Plan)**
- Client-facing role with async-only communication
- Advisory role with no people-oriented Belbin
- Technical role with Attention to Detail < Level 3
- Manager with no leadership Belbin

### **Medium (Monitor & Develop)**
- Advisory role with avoiding conflict style
- Technical role with no specialist Belbin
- Various skill/role misalignments

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### "No data showing"
- Ensure team members have completed assessments
- Check that `is_active = true` for members
- Verify assessment data exists in database

### "Scores seem low"
- Remember: 60-79 is "Good" not "Bad"
- Hybrid requires BOTH Advisory AND Technical ≥60
- Leadership requires senior role + high EQ

### "Calculation takes too long"
- Normal for 10+ team members
- Each member requires 6 database queries
- Should complete within 60 seconds
